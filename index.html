Frequently Asked Questions

What does the bot actually do?

It reads your inbound dispatch emails, evaluates the trip details against your specific rules, and replies instantly to claim trips that match your criteria, securing them before competitors do.

Does it replace dispatch?

No. It gives your dispatchers leverage. It acts as a lightning-fast filter that handles the initial land-grab for profitable trips, letting your humans focus on actual logistics and edge cases.

How fast can I get started?

Onboarding usually takes less than 48 hours. Once you submit your preferences and set up your email forwarding, we deploy your bot.

Can it be tailored to my trip preferences?

Yes. You provide your operational logic (e.g., specific zip codes, minimum miles, wheelchair vs. ambulatory) and the bot only acts on trips that fit your parameters.
